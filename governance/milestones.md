## 2026

### Confirmed
- **Mar 16** — Luminis Foundation incorporated in New Mexico (Entity ID 0008089293); Certificate of Incorporation issued, effective Mar 16.
- **Mar 19** — Organizational meeting of the Board of Directors; bylaws and conflict-of-interest policy adopted; officers elected (Carlos Garcia, President; Guillermo Martin, Secretary; Adam Kimble, Treasurer).

- **Jun 10** — Awesome Foundation Conservation & Climate microgrant application submitted for MycoSense.
